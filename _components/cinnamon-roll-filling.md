title:  "Cinnamon Rolls Filling"

ingredients:
- ½ cup butter
- ½ cup sugar
- ½ brown sugar
- 6 tablespoons all-purpose flour
- 1 ½ tablespoons ground cinnamon

directions:
- Soften butter
- Add ingredients to medium bowl 
- Mix with electric mixer
