ingredients:

- 1 tbsp chili powder
- 1 tsp cumin ground
- 1 tsp paprika (I used smoked)
- 1/4 tsp cayenne pepper
- 1 tsp salt
- 1 tsp ground black pepper

directions:

- Mix all the ingredients together in a bowl. Recipe will yield about 2 1/2 tbsp. Store in a spice jar in your spice cabinet.
