ingredients:
- ⅓ cups olive oil
- ⅓ cups apple cider vinegar
- 1 Tbsp. apple cider
- 1 ½ Tbsp. honey
- ¼ tsp. pepper
- ¼ tsp. ginger

directions:
-  combine all ingredients and shake vigorously.
