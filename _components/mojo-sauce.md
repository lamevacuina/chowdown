ingredients:
- 1 cup chicken stock
- 1 head of garlic, minced
- ½ cup fresh orange juice
- ¼ cup fresh lime juice
- 1 tsp dried oregano
- 1 tsp ground cumin
- 1 tsp salt
- ½ tsp freshly-cracked black pepper

directions:
- Whisk all ingredients together in a medium mixing bowl until combined
