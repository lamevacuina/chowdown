recipeYield: 2 dozen

ingredients:
- 1/2  Cup shortening
- 1/2  Cup butter
- 1/2 Cup sugar
- 1/2 Cup brown sugar
- 1 egg
- 1 Teaspoon vanilla
- 1 1/2 Cups flour
- 1/2 Teaspoon baking soda
- 1/2 Teaspoon salt
- 1/2 Bag chocolate chips
- 1/2 Cup nuts (optional)

directions:
- Preheat oven to 375
- Mix fats and sugars
- Add in Egg, vanilla and dry ingredients
- Mix in chocolate chips
- Bake for 8-10 minutes
